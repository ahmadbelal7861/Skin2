## Bilinear CNN using a matrix-similarity based joint loss function for skin disease classification.
## Dataset:
The ISIC-2019 dataset used for model training can be downloaded from https://challenge.isic-archive.com/data/#2019.
The HAM10000 dataset employed in this study can be accessed from the Kaggle dataset repository: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/kmader/skin-cancer-mnist-ham10000.
The private dataset used in this study was obtained from a hospital and contains sensitive, privately held clinical information. Due to confidentiality and institutional regulations, the dataset cannot be shared publicly. However, the authors may provide controlled access to the data upon receiving formal approval from the source hospital and after ensuring all ethical and legal requirements are met. Interested researchers may contact the corresponding author to initiate the approval process.
